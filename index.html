Menu Memories

Meals are more than food, but about the memories associated with it. From familys to single people, every bite reminds you of life.
Now, what nobody told me is that as an adult you would spend the rest of your life eating your decisions. Everyday you have to make a choice of what to have for dinner. Sometimes, you fall into a rut and end up having the same meals over and over.
The purpose of this page and app is to add some variety. Each day has a button for you to click. This will give you a menu with recipes for that day. The calendar itself is done on a weekly basis. On the right a grocery list is generated based on the menu.
